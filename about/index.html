About

Donatas Navidonskis is a highly experienced Staff Software Engineer with a strong background in a wide range of technical areas, including cloud computing, cloud architecture, automation, and containerization. As a certified Google Cloud Platform (GCP) engineer , he has a proven track record of leading teams in an Agile environment and a focus on delivering robust, reliable systems.

Professional Biography

Donatas has a wealth of experience in Site Reliability Engineering, including leading and managing teams. He played a key role in the company's successful infrastructure migration from dedicated servers to the Google Cloud Platform. He also helped achieve zero downtime during the Black Friday 2020 campaigns, which resulted in a company-wide bonus for all employees. Donatas is dedicated to driving continuous improvement and is a proactive team player. He is proficient in languages like Lithuanian and English and holds a Bachelor's degree in network and system administration.

Based in Vilnius, Lithuania.
